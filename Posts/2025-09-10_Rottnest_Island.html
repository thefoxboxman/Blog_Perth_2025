Wadjemup - Rottnest Island

Wadjemup (Rottnest Island )is half hour ferry ride from the Fremantle port. Thankfull the sky has cleared and the winds are very calm. So off we go in search of Quokka.

We take the hop-on-hop-off bus around the island. The scenary is impressive.

We looked everywhere for Quokkas on the on the 25km island loop, but to no avail. Then we found one. Then we found dozens. They were feeding under the Moreton Bay Figs and the cafe tables.
