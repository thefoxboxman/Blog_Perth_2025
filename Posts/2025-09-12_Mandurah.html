Mandurah

Technically Perth's most southern suburb. Very much the vibe of a coastal holiday destination like the Sunshine Coast in Queensland. Ocean front, white sand surfing beaches, lakes, waterways and canal estates.

Pretty close to the ideal Australian regional city. Great roads, great public facilities, great public transport and very little traffic.

Ocean front surf beachs as far as the eye can see.
